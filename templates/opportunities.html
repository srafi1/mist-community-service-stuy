STUDENT OPPORTUNITIES

Diploma Disparity

OVERVIEW & PURPOSE

        The American education system is accessible to all students, regardless of economic or social status, so that everyone may gain a rewarding education. However, only 53% of New York City public high schools have sufficient college and career programs. In addition, according to statistics released by the NYC Department of Education, some schools have a graduation rate as low as 28% while others boast a full 100%. This disparity may be explained by the lack of access to resources such as college and test preparation, tutoring, internship and volunteer opportunities.
